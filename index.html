About Us

Chef Siri

Siri is a seasoned Thai cook with a lifelong passion for creating delicious and authentic dishes. She first discovered her love for cooking at the age of 10 and has been honing her skills ever since. Her expertise has been passed down to her son, whom she began teaching at the tender age of 7. Together, they have cooked for their family-owned restaurant and store, delighting customers with their flavorful creations. Siri's family also owns and operates a successful restaurant in Japan, which has been thriving for over two decades. With her years of experience and dedication to the craft, Siri is a true master of Thai cuisine.

Amazing Thai Food

Our truck is more than just a place to grab lunch. It is a place to try out new dishes created by an amazing Thai chef. The food is created fresh to your order and with authentic flavors of Thailand.
